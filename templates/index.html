Introduction About the Data :

Zomato Time taken delivery Prediction

Aim of Data The goal of the dataset, which is to predict the Time_taken for a given delivery using (Regression Analysis).

  1. ID : unique identifier of each Zomato record or item in the dataset.
  2. Delivery_person_ID : Delivery_person_ID refers to the unique ID that the delivery person gets when they receive an order from Zomato.
  3. Delivery_person_Age : Delivery person age refers to the age of the person who is delivering the order
  4. Delivery_person_Ratings : Delivery person Ratings refers to the Ratings of the person who is delivering the order
  5. Restaurant_latitude : Restaurant latitude refers to the latitude of the location of the restaurant where the order is placed.
  6. Restaurant_longitude : Restaurant longitude refers to the longitude of the location of the restaurant where the order is placed.
  7. Delivery_location_latitude : Delivery location latitude refers to the latitude of the location where the order is being delivered.
  8. Delivery_location_longitude : Delivery location longitude refers to the longitude of the location where the order is being delivered.
  9. Order_Date : Order Date" refers to the date when the order was placed.
  10. Time_Orderd :Time Ordered refers to the time when the order was placed
  11. Time_Order_picked : Time Order Picked refers to the time when the delivery person picked up the order from the restaurant.
  12. Weather_conditions : Weather Conditions refers to the atmospheric conditions at the time of delivery.
  13. Road_traffic_density : Road traffic density refers to the amount of traffic on the road at the time of delivery, which can affect the delivery time.
  14. Vehicle_condition : Vehicle condition refers to the state of the delivery vehicle, such as its maintenance, fuel level, and cleanliness
  15. Type_of_order : Type of order refers to the categorization of orders based on their attributes, such as the type of cuisine, the size of the order, and the delivery method
  16. Type_of_vehicle : Type of vehicle refers to the type of vehicle used for the delivery.
  17. multiple_deliveries : Multiple deliveries refers to the situation where a delivery person has to deliver multiple orders at the same time or in quick succession.
  18. Festival : Festival refers to whether there is a festival or not during the time of delivery
  19. City : City refers to the city where the order is placed.

    Target variable:

  1. Time_taken (min) : Time_taken (min) refers to the time taken to deliver the order from the restaurant to the delivery location.
